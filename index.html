About us

A foundation built on care, curiosity, and community

Nestled in King Williams Town in the Eastern Cape, Amilak Educational Services is a school that sees every learner as an individual — not a number. We offer a structured yet nurturing environment where children from Grade R to Grade 5 develop confidence, literacy, and a lifelong love of learning.

Our extended hours from 6:00 AM to 5:00 PM mean working families can trust that their children are safe, engaged, and growing — every single day.

Safe & nurturing
A protected, welcoming space where every child feels seen and supported throughout the school day.
Strong curriculum
Aligned to the South African curriculum, with a focus on foundational literacy, numeracy, and creative growth.
Community-centred
We partner with families across King Williams Town to raise well-rounded, confident young people.
